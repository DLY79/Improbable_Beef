The goal of Improbable Beef is to create a burger using bacteria found in the navels of our test volunteers. You've each been assigned an unique identifier, or "Test Subject ID No." Using the drop down menu on the left hand side of this page, find your number to discover what your top 10 bacteria cultures are. You will also notice that we have included other visualizations below related to the frequency of which you scrub your belly button as well as one that indicates the variety of bacteria found. In this bubble chart, the larger the circle, the greater the amount of that specific culture. Have questions or notice an error? Don't hesitate to send mail.
